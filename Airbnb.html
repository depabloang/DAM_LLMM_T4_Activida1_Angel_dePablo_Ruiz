About us

Airbnb was born in 2007 when two Hosts welcomed three guests to their San Francisco home, and has since grown to 4 million Hosts who have welcomed more than 1 billion guest arrivals in almost every country across the globe. Every day, Hosts offer unique stays and one-of-a-kind activities that make it possible for guests to experience the world in a more authentic, connected way.

The Airbnb story



October 2007

Brian and Joe host Airbnb & Breakfast's first guests

March 2008

Airbed & Breakfast officially launches during SXSW. Two bookings!

August 2008

The Airbed & Breakfast website launches in time for the Democratic National Convention and gets 80 bookings

August 2008

We launch Payents, our bespoke payments platform which in 2019 processed about $70 billion in guest and Host transactions in over 40 currencies
